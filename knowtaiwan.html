Know More About Taiwan

Taiwan is a small tropical/subtropical island in East Asia, often called Formosa ("Beautiful Island"). Despite its size, it's rich in mountains, sea views, night markets, and world-class technology. Like Ireland, Taiwan is a proud island society with a warm, welcoming culture.

Quick Q&A

Q1: Why is Taiwan also called "Formosa"?

In the 16th century, Portuguese sailors wrote "Ilha Formosa," meaning "Beautiful Island." The name became a poetic nickname still used today.
